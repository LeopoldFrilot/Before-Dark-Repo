    • The Sun is Setting, It's Time to Go Home

    Our hero is a boy with a big imagination in a small neighborhood. After playing outside all day he has to get home before sunset. In order to do this he'll have to fight monsters and search for tools so he can overcome the final boss, an evil shadow monster.


    Gameplay

    The gameplay is centered around searching for a sword in order to fight the final boss. The sword will be hidden in a random location for replayability, and in searching for it the player will have to fight various monsters and do some platforming. In designing the gameplay we attempted to go for an action adventure style where players can replay multiple times and, with our random elements, have a different experience each time.
